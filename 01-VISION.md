## Non-Goals (Explicit Boundaries)

1. **Not a replacement for observability tools** - We integrate, not replace
2. **Not a no-code platform** - Engineers need to understand what's happening
3. **Not fully autonomous** - Humans remain in the loop for high-risk actions
4. **Not a silver bullet** - Good practices still matter
